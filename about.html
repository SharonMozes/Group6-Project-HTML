About Us

Welcome to Fairy Floss Delight

At Fairy Floss Delight, we're not just a bakery; we're artisans of delectable experiences.
From the moment you step through our doors,
you're greeted with the irresistible aroma of freshly baked treats and the warmth of our friendly staff.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Fairy Floss Delight began as a dream shared by RTC G6 to create a haven where sweetness and creativity collide.
With a passion for baking and a dedication to quality, our founder set out to redefine the dessert experience.
What started as a humble kitchen experiment quickly blossomed into a beloved local bakery, cherished by the community for our commitment to excellence.
